## üõ† Troubleshooting Tips

If Handora does not work as expected, try the following:

- **Webcam Not Detected:** Make sure your webcam is connected and not used by another program.
- **No Video Feed:** Refresh your browser or restart the app. Check browser permissions to allow webcam use.
- **Tracking is Slow:** Close other heavy apps or browser tabs. Try again in a well-lit room.
- **Hand or Face Not Recognized:** Position yourself and your hands closer to the camera and ensure they are fully visible.
- **Game Controls Not Responding:** Make sure gestures are clear and deliberate. Check if the app is focused and receiving inputs.
